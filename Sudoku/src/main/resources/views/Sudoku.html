Sudoku



Play an online version of the classic game





The objective is to fill a 9 × 9 grid with digits so that each column, each row, and each of the nine 3 × 3 subgrids that compose the grid contain all of the digits from 1 to 9.

Take notes of what values could possibly be in the cell. Remove values from a cell as you rule them out to keep a note of the potential values for the cell.

Highlight all the mistakes you have made and highlight new mistakes while you play.

Easily undo all moves you make and if you go back too far you can also redo them.

Get a number placed in the right position for you. You get infinite hints but only use them if you need to cause getting stuck and thinking it through is part of the fun.
